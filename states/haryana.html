Top 10 Places to Visit in Haryana

Kurukshetra

Historic city associated with the Mahabharata and Bhagavad Gita.

Read moreVisit Brahma Sarovar, Jyotisar, and the Gita Gyan Sansthanam to explore its spiritual significance.

Pinjore Gardens

Mughal-style terraced gardens perfect for picnics and evening strolls.

Read moreAlso known as Yadavindra Gardens, they feature fountains, temples, and a mini zoo.

Morni Hills

The only hill station in Haryana offering nature trails and lake views.

Read moreGreat for trekking, bird watching, and boating. A peaceful weekend getaway.

Surajkund

Home to the famous Surajkund Mela and ancient sun temple ruins.

Bhima Devi Temple Complex

Read moreKnown as the Khajuraho of North India for its erotic art and historic significance.

Badkhal Lake

A serene lake near Faridabad ideal for boating and relaxing.

Read moreSurrounded by the Aravalli Hills, it's a popular spot for nature lovers and photographers.

Sultanpur Bird Sanctuary

A paradise for bird watchers located near Gurugram.

Read moreHosts over 250 bird species, both migratory and resident, especially during winters.

Kingdom of Dreams

A grand entertainment complex in Gurugram showcasing Indian arts and culture.

Read moreEnjoy theatrical performances, food courts, and live music in this cultural hotspot.

Bhishma Kund

Legendary site in Narwana linked to Bhishma Pitamah of Mahabharata.

Read moreA peaceful pond said to have appeared miraculously for Bhishma to rest.

Karnal

Historic town known as the city of 'Daanveer Karna' with colonial and Mughal heritage.

Read moreExplore Karnal Fort, Cantonment Church Tower, and Karna Lake for a cultural experience.

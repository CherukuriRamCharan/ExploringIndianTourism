Top 10 Places to Visit in Mizoram

Aizawl

The capital city Aizawl is the cultural as well as political center of Mizoram.

Serchhip

Home to the tallest waterfalls in the state, Serchhip is heaven for lovers of nature.

Lunglei

This is a pretty town, with views and surroundings so peaceful that one forgets about all the worldly issues.

Champhai

It is a picturesque town on the Indo-Myanmar border.

Reiek >

A quaint village located about 29 km from Aizawl, Reiek is perfect for a day trip.

Mamit For wildlife

The Dampa Tiger Reserve, located here, is the largest wildlife sanctuary in Mizoram.

Kolasib Situated

the Assam border, Kolasib is known for its tranquil environment and scenic beauty.

Hmuifang

Hmuifang is a hill station famous for its verdant landscapes and adventure activities.

Saiha

Saiha is a haven for birdwatchers and nature enthusiasts

Phawngpui

Popularly known as the Blue Mountain, Phawngpui is the highest peak in Mizoram.

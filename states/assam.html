Top 10 Places to visit in Assam

Kamakhya Temple

Ancient Shakti Peetha atop Nilachal Hill in Guwahati.

Read moreDedicated to Goddess Kamakhya, it's one of the most revered shrines in India.

Rang Ghar

Asia's oldest surviving amphitheater located in Sivasagar.

Read moreBuilt by Ahom kings, it was used for royal sports and cultural events.

Talatal Ghar

Historic palace with underground tunnels in Sivasagar.

Read moreConstructed by the Ahom dynasty, it showcases unique architectural features.

Umananda Temple

Island temple dedicated to Lord Shiva on the Brahmaputra River.

Read moreAccessible by boat, it's known for its serene environment and spiritual significance.

Agnigarh

Hilltop fortress in Tezpur associated with mythological tales.

Read moreBelieved to be the site where Princess Usha was kept in isolation by her father.

Madan Kamdev

Archaeological site with ruins of ancient temples near Guwahati.

Read moreFeatures intricate carvings and sculptures dating back to the 9th-10th centuries.

Surya Pahar

Archaeological site in Goalpara with numerous rock-cut sculptures.

Read moreKnown for its association with sun worship and ancient relics.

Khaspur

Ruins of the Dimasa kingdom near Silchar.

Read moreFeatures remnants of palaces, temples, and ancient gateways.

Charaideo Maidams

Royal burial mounds of Ahom kings in Charaideo.

Read moreConsidered the 'Pyramids of Assam', they reflect the rich funerary architecture of the Ahom dynasty.

Barpeta Satra

Important Vaishnavite monastery in Barpeta.

Read moreFounded by Srimanta Sankardeva's disciple, it remains a spiritual and cultural hub of Assamese heritage.

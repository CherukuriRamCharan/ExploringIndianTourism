Top 10 Places to Visit in Rajasthan

Jaipur

The Pink City with iconic forts and palaces.

Read more Visit Hawa Mahal, City Palace, Amer Fort, Jantar Mantar and enjoy vibrant bazaars.

Udaipur

The City of Lakes known for romance and royalty.

Read more Explore Lake Pichola, City Palace, Jag Mandir, and enjoy a sunset boat ride.

Jodhpur

The Blue City, rich in heritage and forts.

Read more Visit Mehrangarh Fort, Jaswant Thada, Umaid Bhawan Palace, and enjoy traditional Rajasthani cuisine.

Jaisalmer

The Golden City with desert magic and sandstone forts.

Read more See Jaisalmer Fort, Patwon Ki Haveli, enjoy desert safaris and cultural shows.

Mount Abu

Rajasthan’s only hill station with cool climate and scenic views.

Read more Visit Nakki Lake, Dilwara Temples, Sunset Point, and relax in nature.

Bikaner

Known for forts, temples, and camel safaris.

Read more Explore Junagarh Fort, Karni Mata (Rat) Temple, Lalgarh Palace, and Bikaneri bhujia.

Pushkar

A holy town famous for its lake and camel fair.

Read more Visit Brahma Temple, Pushkar Lake, take a dip, and explore local bazaars.

Chittorgarh

A historic fort city symbolizing Rajput valor.

Read more Explore Chittorgarh Fort, Vijay Stambh, Rani Padmini’s Palace, and temples.

Ajmer

Home to the famous Ajmer Sharif Dargah.

Read more Visit the dargah of Khwaja Moinuddin Chishti and Ana Sagar Lake. A spiritual experience.

Bundi

Charming town with palaces, stepwells, and murals.

Read more See Taragarh Fort, Bundi Palace, Rani Ji Ki Baori, and admire the medieval vibe.

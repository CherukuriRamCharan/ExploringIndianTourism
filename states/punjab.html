Top 10 Places to Visit in Punjab

Amritsar

Home to the Golden Temple, the spiritual center of Sikhism.

Read more Visit Jallianwala Bagh, Wagah Border, and savor authentic Punjabi cuisine in the historic city.

Chandigarh

A well-planned city with gardens, lakes, and modern architecture.

Read more Explore Sukhna Lake, Rock Garden, Rose Garden, and shop at Sector 17 Plaza.

Patiala

City of royal heritage, palaces, and traditional Punjabi culture.

Read more Discover Qila Mubarak, Sheesh Mahal, and the famous Patiala peg and jutti craftsmanship.

Anandpur Sahib

One of the most sacred places in Sikhism.

Read more Visit Takht Sri Kesgarh Sahib, Virasat-e-Khalsa museum, and experience Hola Mohalla festival.

Wagah Border

Famous for the Beating Retreat Ceremony held daily at sunset.

Read more Witness patriotic fervor and high-energy performance by Indian and Pakistani soldiers at the border.

Fatehgarh Sahib

Important Sikh pilgrimage site with deep historical significance.

Read more It commemorates the martyrdom of Guru Gobind Singh’s sons. A peaceful and sacred destination.

Kapurthala

Often called the “Paris of Punjab” for its French-inspired architecture.

Read more Explore Jagatjit Palace, Moorish Mosque, and the scenic Pushpa Gujral Science City nearby.

Sultanpur Lodhi

Where Guru Nanak Dev Ji attained enlightenment.

Read more Visit Gurudwara Ber Sahib and the tranquil ghats along the Kali Bein River.

Ropar Wetland

A scenic man-made freshwater wetland perfect for birdwatching and nature walks.

Read more Spot migratory birds, take nature photos, and explore the rich biodiversity near Rupnagar.

Hoshiarpur

A lesser-known gem known for its temples, hills, and handicrafts.

Read more Visit Takhni-Rehmapur Wildlife Sanctuary, local temples, and buy traditional wooden inlay work.

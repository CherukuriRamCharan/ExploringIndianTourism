Krang Suri Falls

Krang Suri Falls in Meghalaya is one of the stunning waterfalls. It offers breathtaking views and lush green landscapes. Krang Suri Falls is the right choice if you want a peaceful escape from your life.

Garden of Caves

Garden of Caves is suitable for all nature and peace lovers looking for a place to visit in Meghalaya.

Nohsngithiang (Seven Sisters) Falls

Nihsngithiang Falls in Meghalaya is one of the beautiful waterfalls located near Cherrapunji.

Wei Sawdong

Embrace a peaceful retreat by visiting Wei Sawdong, a beautiful waterfall in Meghalaya.

Krem Liat Prah

Krem Liat Prah is one of Meghalaya’s most beautiful root bridges. It is surrounded by lush green forests made from the roots of rubber trees and created by the Khasi tribes.

Khasi Hills

These hills offer a lush green landscape filled with green valleys.

Garo Hills

Garo Hills in Meghalaya are known for their rich culture and greenery, making them one of the best places to visit.

Lady Hydari Park

Lady Hydari Park is located in the capital of Meghalaya. It is a beautiful park with a large garden and beautiful flowers.

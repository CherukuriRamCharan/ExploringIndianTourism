Top 10 Places to Visit in Gujarat

Rann of Kutch

A vast white salt desert famous for the Rann Utsav and stunning landscapes.

Read moreThe Rann comes alive during the full moon and cultural festivities. Don’t miss the camel rides and handicrafts.

Somnath Temple

One of the 12 Jyotirlingas and a major Hindu pilgrimage site.

Read moreThis majestic temple stands on the Arabian Sea and symbolizes resilience through centuries of invasions and reconstructions.

Gir National Park

The only place in the world where Asiatic lions roam freely in the wild.

Read moreTake a jungle safari and spot leopards, deer, and birds alongside lions in their natural habitat.

Statue of Unity

The tallest statue in the world dedicated to Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel.

Read moreVisit the museum, viewing gallery, and enjoy boating and light shows around the statue complex near Kevadia.

Saputara

A hill station nestled in the Western Ghats known for its scenic beauty.

Read morePerfect for boating, cable car rides, and visiting tribal museums and gardens.

Modhera Sun Temple

An architectural marvel built by the Solanki dynasty in the 11th century.

Read moreDedicated to the Sun God, this temple is known for its intricate carvings and stepwell (Surya Kund).

Dwarka

One of the Char Dhams and a sacred city associated with Lord Krishna.

Read moreVisit Dwarkadhish Temple, Bet Dwarka island, and take a dip at Gomti Ghat.

Sabarmati Ashram

Gandhiji's residence and a key site in India’s freedom movement.

Read moreExplore the peaceful ashram, museum, and learn about Mahatma Gandhi’s life and ideologies.

Palitana Temples

A sacred Jain pilgrimage site with 800+ temples atop Shatrunjaya Hill.

Read moreThe trek up the 3,500 steps is a spiritual journey with stunning architectural wonders.

Vadodara

A cultural city with royal heritage and the grand Laxmi Vilas Palace.

Read moreAlso explore Sayaji Gardens, Baroda Museum, and enjoy Navratri celebrations with Garba.

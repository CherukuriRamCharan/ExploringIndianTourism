Top 10 Places to Visit in Sikkim

Tsomgo Lake

A stunning glacial lake surrounded by snow-capped mountains.

Read more Also called Changu Lake, it's located at 12,000 ft and changes color with the seasons.

Rumtek Monastery

One of the most important Buddhist monasteries in India.

Read more Seat of the Karmapa Lama, this monastery showcases Tibetan architecture and spiritual heritage.

Nathula Pass

A high-altitude Indo-China border pass.

Read more Located on the old Silk Route, you can witness Indo-China border trade posts here.

Yuksom

The historic capital and gateway to trekking routes.

Read more Visit Norbugang Coronation Throne and explore beautiful trails like Dzongri-Goecha La.

Gurudongmar Lake

One of the highest lakes in the world.

Read more Sacred to both Buddhists and Sikhs, it's located at 17,800 ft in North Sikkim.

Gangtok

The lively capital city of Sikkim.

Read more Explore MG Marg, Enchey Monastery, cable car rides, and enjoy delicious Tibetan cuisine.

Pelling

Offers panoramic views of Kanchenjunga and scenic landscapes.

Read more Visit Pemayangtse Monastery, Rabdentse Ruins, and Sky Walk with glass bridge.

Zuluk

A remote hamlet with breathtaking hairpin bends and misty hills.

Read more Famous for the Zig-Zag Road and great views of Kanchenjunga.

Lachung

Picturesque mountain village and base for Yumthang Valley.

Read more Surrounded by apple orchards, waterfalls, and is a springboard to the Valley of Flowers.

Yumthang Valley

Known as the Valley of Flowers of the East.

Read more Located at 11,800 ft, this valley blooms with rhododendrons and alpine flowers during spring.

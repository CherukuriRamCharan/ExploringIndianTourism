Top 10 Places to Visit in Odisha

Puri

Home to the sacred Jagannath Temple and famous Rath Yatra.

Read more Enjoy Puri Beach, visit Gundicha Temple and explore local markets for Odisha handicrafts and sweets.

Konark

Renowned for the UNESCO-listed Sun Temple.

Read more Explore the incredible stone chariot architecture and the nearby Chandrabhaga Beach.

Bhubaneswar

The Temple City of India with rich heritage and urban charm.

Read more Visit Lingaraj Temple, Udayagiri and Khandagiri Caves, and Odisha State Museum.

Chilika Lake

Asia’s largest brackish water lagoon and bird sanctuary.

Read more Take a boat ride to spot dolphins and migratory birds, and visit Kalijai Temple on the island.

Simlipal National Park

Biodiverse national park with tigers, elephants, and waterfalls.

Read more Trek through dense forests, enjoy Barehipani and Joranda Falls, and explore tribal villages.

Hirakud Dam

One of the world’s longest earthen dams, built across the Mahanadi River.

Read more Enjoy scenic views, boating, and nearby wildlife sanctuaries like Debrigarh.

Gopalpur

Serene beach town ideal for a peaceful seaside vacation.

Read more Relax on the quiet beaches, explore colonial architecture, and indulge in seafood.

Dhauli

Historical site linked with Ashoka’s transformation to Buddhism.

Read more Visit the Peace Pagoda (Shanti Stupa) and ancient rock edicts by Emperor Ashoka.

Ratnagiri

Part of the Buddhist Diamond Triangle with ancient monasteries.

Read more Explore impressive Buddhist sculptures, stupas, and peaceful archaeological sites.

Cuttack

Historic city famous for silver filigree work and Barabati Fort.

Read more Walk through the medieval fort ruins, visit local bazaars, and enjoy delicious Odisha snacks.

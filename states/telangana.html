Top 10 Places to Visit in Telangana

Charminar

Iconic monument and mosque in the heart of Hyderabad.

Read more Built in 1591, it is surrounded by lively markets and is a symbol of Hyderabad’s culture.

Golconda Fort

Majestic citadel with rich history and amazing acoustics.

Read more Once famous for diamonds, the fort is known for its sound engineering and panoramic views.

Ramoji Film City

One of the world’s largest film studio complexes.

Read more A must-visit for movie lovers with sets, gardens, and interactive tours.

Birla Mandir

Peaceful white marble temple with city views.

Read more Located on Naubat Pahad, this temple is dedicated to Lord Venkateshwara.

Warangal

Historic city known for Kakatiya architecture.

Read more Key sites include Warangal Fort, Thousand Pillar Temple, and the grand stone gateways (Kakatiya Kala Thoranam).

Nagarjuna Sagar

Massive dam and scenic reservoir on the Krishna River.

Read more Also visit the island museum at Nagarjunakonda with Buddhist relics and history.

Bhadrakali Temple

One of the oldest temples of Goddess Bhadrakali in Warangal.

Read more Set by a lake, the temple is known for its serene atmosphere and annual festivals.

Yadadri Temple

Beautifully rebuilt hilltop temple near Hyderabad.

Read more Dedicated to Lord Narasimha, the temple complex is modern yet deeply spiritual.

Medak Church

Gothic-style cathedral with stained-glass artwork.

Read more One of the largest churches in Asia, built during British rule with unique architecture.

Kuntala Falls

The highest waterfall in Telangana nestled in the forest.

Read more A scenic spot near Adilabad, perfect for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

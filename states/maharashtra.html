Top 10 Places to Visit in Maharashtra

Ellora Caves

The invaluable ensemble of 34 caves at Ellora in the Charanandri hills of western India's Maharashtra State

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus

The Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus, formerly known as Victoria Terminus Station, in Mumbai

Shree Siddhivinayak Temple

The Shri Siddhivinayak Ganapati Mandir is a Hindu temple dedicated to Ganesha

Chhatrapati Sambhaji Nagar

Chh.Sambhajinagar is the fifth-most populous urban area in Maharashtra after Mumbai, Pune, Nagpur

Shirdi

Shree Saibaba Sansthan Trust, Shirdi, is the Governing and Administrative body of Shri Saibaba's Samadhi Temple and all others temples in this premises

Kolhapur

Kolhapur is one of the most significant cities in South Maharashtra and has been a hub of historical, religious, and cultural activities for centuries.

Bhandardara

Bhandardara hill station is nestled in the Sahyadri ranges of Maharashtra.

Bhimashankar Temple

Bhimashankar Temple is a Hindu temple dedicated to Shiva situated in its eponymous village, Bhimashankar, in Pune district of Maharashtra.

Ganpatipule

The 400 year old Ganpati idol at Ganpatipule is said to have sprung up from the earth.

Tarkarli

Tarkarli is a village in the western Indian state of Maharashtra.

Top 10 Places to Visit in Uttar Pradesh

Agra

Home to the iconic Taj Mahal, Agra Fort, and the Mughal legacy.

Read more Agra also features Itmad-ud-Daulah's Tomb and Mehtab Bagh. Visit Sadar Bazaar for local shopping and taste the famous Agra Petha.

Varanasi

The spiritual heart of India with sacred Ganga ghats and temples.

Read more Don’t miss the Ganga Aarti and a boat ride at sunrise. Explore the lanes of Varanasi filled with old-world charm and spiritual energy.

Sarnath

Where Buddha gave his first sermon after enlightenment.

Read more Explore the Dhamek Stupa, Sarnath Museum, Ashokan Pillar, and learn about ancient Buddhist teachings and culture.

Ayodhya

Spiritual city and birthplace of Lord Rama.

Read more Visit the Shri Ram Janmbhoomi Mandir, Hanuman Garhi, and explore the sacred ghats and historic temples of this divine city.

Fatehpur Sikri

Architectural marvel built by Emperor Akbar.

Read more Key highlights include Buland Darwaza, Diwan-i-Khas, and Salim Chishti's Tomb, showcasing Mughal artistic brilliance.

Lucknow

The City of Nawabs known for its culture and cuisine.

Read more Discover Bara and Chhota Imambaras, Rumi Darwaza, Hazratganj market, and feast on kebabs, biryanis, and kulfis.

Kushinagar

Where Buddha attained Mahaparinirvana.

Read more A peaceful town with ancient stupas, Buddhist monasteries, and tranquil spots for meditation and learning.

Mathura

Birthplace of Lord Krishna and a vibrant spiritual town.

Read more Visit Krishna Janmabhoomi Temple, Dwarkadhish Temple, and enjoy the energetic Holi festival and Yamuna Aarti.

Vrindavan

Town of temples and devotion to Lord Krishna.

Read more Highlights include Banke Bihari Temple, ISKCON Temple, and the stunning Prem Mandir, especially at night.

Jhansi

Historic town known for Rani Lakshmibai’s bravery.

Read more Explore the Jhansi Fort, Rani Mahal, and the museum that preserves the city's heroic past and 1857 revolt.
